About
Bahrain is an artisanal bakery based in Jordan. Bahrain provides warmth through fresh bread of the finest types of goods for daily use. The entire strategy of the brand’s vision depends on this basis, from where the term “Bahrain” came in the old style, an Arabic word that suggests expansion, and abundant goodness
The appearance of the brand and logo of Bahrain Bakery came to convey some sincere memories of an old bakery in its modern shape with a touch using an elegant logo and an attractive visual hobby that goes in line with its modern shape to maintain its elegant shape that distinguishes it from others and which makes it one of the best bakeries in the region
